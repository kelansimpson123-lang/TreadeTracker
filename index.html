HOW TO SIZE YOUR TRADE
1.Check ForexFactory — red folder USD news? Do not trade.
2.Set risk: 0.5% or 1% max. Total daily cap is 3%.
3.Measure SL distance in ticks on TradingView.
4.MES: ticks × $1.25. MNQ: ticks × $0.50 = cost per contract.
5.Dollar Risk ÷ cost per contract = contracts. Round DOWN.
6.Already traded today? Close the platform and leave.
THE TJR ICT SETUP
1
Mark Key Levels
PDH / PDL · Hourly unswept highs & lows · 30min session vertical lines + H/L · Asia High/Low
2
Identify HTF Liquidity Pool
Where is the nearest unswept LQ above/below? Which pool is price most likely to draw to? This is your bias.
3
Wait for HTF LQ Sweep
Price must sweep the HTF LQ pool (PDH/PDL, hourly H/L, 30min session H/L). No sweep = no trade. Patience is the edge.
4
5 Minute BOS + Confluence
After the sweep, wait for a clean 5min BOS in the opposite direction. Must have 5min confluence — IFVG, SMT, or strong displacement.
5
1 Minute BOS — Entry Trigger
Drop to 1min. Wait for the 1min BOS. This is your entry trigger. Do NOT enter before it fires. No exceptions.
6
Session & Timing Check
London (03:00–08:00 EST) — Scenario 3 only. NY Open (09:30 EST / 14:30 BST) — all scenarios. Entry within 1.5hrs of NY open. Be at desk by 14:00 BST.
7
London End Check
If a H/L swept during London → wait for NY continuation (Scenario 3). If no sweep → wait for level to get hit at NY open, then look for confluences.

03:00–08:00 EST
Scenario 3 ONLY. Asia sweeps HTF LQ and reverses during London — trade the NY continuation in London's direction. Enter early, close before 08:30 EST. Never hold into NY open.
ANALYSIS ONLY
Pre-Market
08:30–09:30 EST
Sit on hands. What did London do? Which scenario is setting up? Be at desk by 14:00 BST. Check ForexFactory for red news.
TRADEABLE — ALL SC
NY Open
09:30 EST / 14:30 BST
Primary session. All 3 scenarios valid. Entry within 1.5hrs — no trade after 11:00 EST / 16:00 BST. Max 0.5–1% risk.
⚠ LONDON TRADE RULE
London trades must be clean, early and closed before 08:30 EST. Never carry a London trade through NY open. If not significantly in profit as Pre-Market approaches — close the trade.
NY SCENARIOS
Scenario 1
Asia creates range → London consolidates within Asia range → NY sweeps HTF LQ → 5min BOS + confluence → 1min BOS entry. Trade NY reversal.
Scenario 2
Asia creates range → London sweeps HTF LQ during London session → NY continues reversal from London sweep → 5min BOS + confluence → 1min BOS entry.
Scenario 3
Asia creates range → London sweeps HTF LQ AND reverses → NY continues in London's direction → 5min BOS + confluence → 1min BOS entry.
⚡ If NY opens near opposing LQ — reassess for fresh manipulation & reversal instead of forcing continuation. Wait. The setup comes to you.
